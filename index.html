Our process

Structured for a lender's review, not just a business exercise.

Every deliverable is written to the format and depth that Canadian lenders expect at the credit adjudication stage.

1

Loan & Lender Discovery

We start by identifying which lenders and programs match your loan type, amount, and stage. Different lenders require different plan formats. We select the target before we write a word.

2

Intake & Brief

A 60-minute call and a structured questionnaire gather everything we need. You review and sign off on the brief before we begin. No open-ended information requests mid-project.

3

Plan Writing & Financial Modeling

Full narrative business plan, three-year financial model, and lender-specific appendices. Cash flow, repayment schedule, and sensitivity analysis are included as standard. Written by a human analyst, reviewed by a second.

4

Submission & Follow-Through

Final PDF and editable Word file, formatted to lender standards. One round of revisions at no charge. We remain available for lender follow-up questions through the credit review period.
